Battlefields are responsible for units' status while units are on the battlefield.

What's in the battlefield object
-the map array  done
-units
-turn queue 
-DOT queue
-temporary  data (data the battlefield needs to calculate damage in an attack, dead units, etc.)

What does the battlefield object do
-handles 'inround' events: attacks, movement(done), death

Specific battlefield methods
-attack takes self, type, and target(position)
-move takes self and destination done.
